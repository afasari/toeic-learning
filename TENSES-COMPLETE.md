## üü¢ PRESENT TENSES

### 1. Present Simple (Present Tense)

#### Rumus
| Subject | Positive | Negative | Question |
|---------|----------|----------|----------|
| I/You/We/They | work | don't work | Do I/you/we/they work? |
| He/She/It | works | doesn't work | Does he/she/it work? |

#### Penggunaan
1. **Facts & General Truths** (fakta & kebenaran umum)
   - Water **boils** at 100¬∞C.
   - The sun **rises** in the east.

2. **Habits & Routines** (kebiasaan & rutinitas)
   - I **wake up** at 6 AM every day.
   - She **goes** to the gym three times a week.

3. **Permanent Situations** (situasi permanen)
   - I **live** in Jakarta.
   - He **works** for a multinational company.

4. **Scheduled Events** (jadwal kegiatan di masa depan)
   - The meeting **starts** at 9 AM tomorrow.
   - The train **leaves** at 5 PM.

5. **Instructions & Directions** (instruksi & arahan)
   - **Mix** the flour and sugar.
   - **Turn left** at the corner.

#### Time Markers (Penanda Waktu)
- always, usually, often, sometimes, rarely, never
- every day/week/month/year
- on Mondays, in the morning, at night

#### Contoh Lengkap
‚úÖ **Positive**:
- The manager **works** in this office.
- Employees **receive** their salary on the 25th.

‚ùå **Negative**:
- I **don't like** coffee.
- She **doesn't understand** the instructions.

‚ùì **Question**:
- **Do** you **speak** English?
- **Does** he **work** here?

### 2. Present Continuous (Present Progressive)

#### Rumus
| Subject | Formula |
|---------|---------|
| I | am + verb-ing |
| He/She/It | is + verb-ing |
| You/We/They | are + verb-ing |

**Negative**: am not / is not (isn't) / are not (aren't) + verb-ing
**Question**: Am/Is/Are + subject + verb-ing?

#### Penggunaan
1. **Actions Happening Now** (aksi sedang terjadi sekarang)
   - I **am reading** this guide.
   - They **are working** on a project.

2. **Temporary Situations** (situasi sementara)
   - I **am staying** with my friend this week.
   - She **is working** at a caf√© for the summer.

3. **Arranged Future Plans** (rencana masa depan yang sudah diatur)
   - We **are meeting** tomorrow at 3 PM.
   - I **am flying** to Jakarta next week.

4. **Annoying or Repetitive Actions** (aksi yang mengganggu atau berulang)
   - He **is always complaining** about work.
   - She **is constantly checking** her phone.

#### Time Markers
- now, at the moment, at present
- currently, these days
- this week/month/year

#### Contoh Lengkap
‚úÖ **Positive**:
- The team **is discussing** the new strategy.
- I **am preparing** for my TOEIC test.

‚ùå **Negative**:
- They **aren't working** today.
- She **isn't feeling** well.

‚ùì **Question**:
- **Are** you **listening** to me?
- **Is** he **coming** to the meeting?

#### Verbs TIDAK bisa digunakan dalam Present Continuous:
- Verbs of thinking: know, understand, believe, think (opinion)
- Verbs of feeling: like, love, hate, want, prefer
- Verbs of possession: have, own, belong
- Verbs of senses: see, hear, smell, taste, feel
- Verbs of existence: be, exist

**Example**: ‚ùå I am knowing the answer. ‚Üí ‚úÖ I know the answer.

### 3. Present Perfect

#### Rumus
| Subject | Formula |
|---------|---------|
| I/You/We/They | have + past participle (V3) |
| He/She/It | has + past participle (V3) |

**Negative**: haven't (have not) / hasn't (has not) + past participle
**Question**: Have/Has + subject + past participle?

#### Penggunaan
1. **Actions Completed in Unspecified Past** (aksi selesai di masa lalu, waktu tidak spesifik)
   - I **have finished** the report. (Tidak disebutkan kapan)
   - She **has visited** Paris.

2. **Experiences in Life** (pengalaman hidup)
   - I **have been** to Bali twice.
   - He **has never seen** snow.

3. **Actions Started in Past & Still Continuing** (aksi mulai di masa lalu & masih berlanjut)
   - I **have lived** here for 10 years. (Masih tinggal di sini)
   - She **has worked** at this company since 2020.

4. **Recent Actions with Present Results** (aksi baru-baru ini, hasil masih terlihat sekarang)
   - I **have lost** my keys. (Sekarang tidak punya kunci)
   - She **has broken** her phone. (Sekarang ponselnya rusak)

#### Time Markers
- just, already, yet
- ever, never
- for (duration), since (starting point)
- recently, lately
- so far, up to now

#### Perbedaan "For" vs "Since"
| For | Since |
|-----|-------|
| Durasi (berapa lama) | Titik awal (kapan mulai) |
| for 2 hours, for 5 days | since 2020, since yesterday |
| for a long time | since last week |

#### Contoh Lengkap
‚úÖ **Positive**:
- I **have completed** the assignment.
- She **has received** the email.

‚ùå **Negative**:
- They **haven't arrived** yet.
- He **hasn't called** me back.

‚ùì **Question**:
- **Have** you **finished** your work?
- **Has** she **submitted** the report?

#### Present Perfect vs Past Simple
| Present Perfect | Past Simple |
|-----------------|-------------|
| Waktu tidak spesifik | Waktu spesifik |
- I have visited Paris. | - I visited Paris in 2019. |
- He has lost his keys. | - He lost his keys yesterday. |

### 4. Present Perfect Continuous

#### Rumus
| Subject | Formula |
|---------|---------|
| I/You/We/They | have + been + verb-ing |
| He/She/It | has + been + verb-ing |

**Negative**: haven't/hasn't + been + verb-ing
**Question**: Have/Has + subject + been + verb-ing?

#### Penggunaan
1. **Actions Started in Past, Still Continuing** (aksi mulai di masa lalu & masih berlangsung)
   - I **have been studying** English for 3 years. (Masih belajar)
   - She **has been working** on this project since morning.

2. **Actions Recently Completed with Present Relevance** (aksi baru selesai, relevan dengan sekarang)
   - I **have been waiting** for you. (You just arrived)
   - They **have been discussing** the proposal. (Discussion just ended)

3. **Temporary Situations with Duration** (situasi sementara dengan durasi)
   - He **has been living** with his parents temporarily.
   - We **have been using** this software for a trial period.

#### Time Markers
- for, since
- all day, all morning, all week
- lately, recently
- how long

#### Contoh Lengkap
‚úÖ **Positive**:
- She **has been working** here since 2020.
- They **have been waiting** for two hours.

‚ùå **Negative**:
- I **haven't been feeling** well lately.
- He **hasn't been sleeping** well.

‚ùì **Question**:
- **How long have** you **been studying**?
- **Has** she **been working** on the report?

#### Present Perfect vs Present Perfect Continuous
| Present Perfect | Present Perfect Continuous |
|-----------------|---------------------------|
- I have read the book. (Selesai) | - I have been reading the book. (Belum selesai atau baru selesai) |
- He has painted the room. (Selesai) | - He has been painting the room. (Proses) |

**Catatan**: Gunakan Present Perfect jika fokus pada HASIL/KESELESAIAN
Gunakan Present Perfect Continuous jika fokus pada PROSES/AKTIVITAS

### 5. Past Simple

#### Rumus
| Subject | Regular Verb | Irregular Verb |
|---------|--------------|---------------|
| All subjects | + ed | V2 (irregular form) |

**Negative**: didn't (did not) + base verb (V1)
**Question**: Did + subject + base verb (V1)?

#### Penggunaan
1. **Completed Actions in the Past** (aksi selesai di masa lalu)
   - I **finished** work at 5 PM yesterday.
   - She **called** me last night.

2. **Series of Past Actions** (serangkaian aksi di masa lalu)
   - He **woke up**, **brushed** his teeth, and **went** to work.
   - We **ate** breakfast, **left** the house, and **arrived** at the office.

3. **Past Habits** (kebiasaan di masa lalu)
   - I **played** soccer every weekend when I was young.
   - She **always visited** her grandmother on Sundays.

4. **Past Facts** (fakta di masa lalu)
   - Shakespeare **lived** in England.
   - The company **started** in 2000.

#### Time Markers (Wajib ada atau jelas dari konteks)
- yesterday, last night/week/month/year
- 2 days/weeks/months/years ago
- in 2020, in the past
- at 3 PM, on Monday, in July

#### Contoh Lengkap
‚úÖ **Positive**:
- I **arrived** at the office at 8 AM.
- She **submitted** the report yesterday.

‚ùå **Negative**:
- They **didn't come** to the meeting.
- He **didn't finish** the task.

‚ùì **Question**:
- **Did** you **call** him?
- **Did** she **receive** the email?

#### Regular vs Irregular Verbs
**Regular Verbs** (tambah -ed):
- work ‚Üí worked, finish ‚Üí finished, start ‚Üí started

**Common Irregular Verbs**:
| Base (V1) | Past (V2) | Past Participle (V3) |
|-----------|-----------|---------------------|
| go | went | gone |
| come | came | come |
| do | did | done |
| see | saw | seen |
| eat | ate | eaten |
| write | wrote | written |
| speak | spoke | spoken |
| take | took | taken |
| give | gave | given |
| make | made | made |

### 6. Past Continuous (Past Progressive)

#### Rumus
| Subject | Formula |
|---------|---------|
| I/He/She/It | was + verb-ing |
| You/We/They | were + verb-ing |

**Negative**: was not (wasn't) / were not (weren't) + verb-ing
**Question**: Was/Were + subject + verb-ing?

#### Penggunaan
1. **Action in Progress at Specific Time in Past** (aksi sedang berlangsung di waktu spesifik di masa lalu)
   - At 3 PM yesterday, I **was working** on the report.
   - She **was sleeping** when I called her.

2. **Interrupted Action** (aksi yang terpotong oleh aksi lain)
   - I **was watching** TV when the phone **rang**.
   - They **were eating** dinner when the guests **arrived**.

3. **Parallel Actions in Past** (dua atau lebih aksi yang terjadi bersamaan)
   - While I **was cooking**, she **was setting** the table.
   - They **were laughing** and **talking** loudly.

4. **Setting the Scene in Stories** (mengatur latar cerita)
   - The sun **was shining**, birds **were singing**, and people **were walking** in the park.

#### Time Markers
- at [specific time] in the past
- while, when
- all day, all morning, all night

#### Contoh Lengkap
‚úÖ **Positive**:
- I **was reading** when you called.
- They **were discussing** the project.

‚ùå **Negative**:
- She **wasn't listening** to the music.
- We **weren't working** yesterday.

‚ùì **Question**:
- **Was** he **sleeping** when you arrived?
- **Were** they **watching** TV?

#### Past Continuous vs Past Simple
| Past Continuous | Past Simple |
|----------------|-------------|
| Aksi sedang berlangsung | Aksi terpotong/terselesaikan |
- I was working when he called. | - He called while I was working. |
- She was reading a book. | - She read the book last night. |

### 7. Past Perfect

#### Rumus
| Subject | Formula |
|---------|---------|
| All subjects | had + past participle (V3) |

**Negative**: hadn't (had not) + past participle
**Question**: Had + subject + past participle?

#### Penggunaan
1. **Action Completed Before Another Past Action** (aksi selesai sebelum aksi masa lalu lain)
   - I **had finished** work before he **arrived**.
   - She **had already eaten** when I **invited** her for dinner.

2. **Actions in Sequence** (aksi berurutan di masa lalu)
   - After I **had completed** the first task, I **started** the second.
   - When she **had checked** the email, she **realized** the mistake.

3. **Unfulfilled Past Wishes/Expectations** (harapan/ekspektasi masa lalu yang tidak tercapai)
   - I **had hoped** to finish early, but I was busy.
   - They **had planned** to travel, but it rained.

#### Time Markers
- before, after, when, by the time
- by [past time]
- already, just

#### Contoh Lengkap
‚úÖ **Positive**:
- She **had already left** when I arrived.
- They **had completed** the project before the deadline.

‚ùå **Negative**:
- I **hadn't finished** by the time he called.
- He **hadn't seen** the movie before.

‚ùì **Question**:
- **Had** you **finished** before the meeting?
- **Had** she **read** the book?

#### Past Perfect vs Past Simple
| Past Perfect | Past Simple |
|--------------|-------------|
| Aksi lebih awal di masa lalu | Aksi setelahnya |
- When I arrived, the train **had already left**. | - The train left 5 minutes ago. |
- After she **had eaten**, she went to work. | - She ate breakfast, then went to work. |

### 8. Past Perfect Continuous

#### Rumus
| Subject | Formula |
|---------|---------|
| All subjects | had + been + verb-ing |

**Negative**: hadn't (had not) + been + verb-ing
**Question**: Had + subject + been + verb-ing?

#### Penggunaan
1. **Action Continuing Up to Another Past Action** (aksi berlanjut sampai aksi masa lalu lain)
   - I **had been working** for 3 hours when he called.
   - She **had been studying** for the exam all day before she took a break.

2. **Cause & Effect in Past** (sebab & akibat di masa lalu)
   - He **had been running**, so he was out of breath.
   - They **had been working** all day, so they were exhausted.

#### Time Markers
- for, since
- before, when
- all day, all morning, all week

#### Contoh Lengkap
‚úÖ **Positive**:
- I **had been waiting** for an hour when she arrived.
- They **had been discussing** the proposal all morning.

‚ùå **Negative**:
- She **hadn't been sleeping** well.
- He **hadn't been exercising** regularly.

‚ùì **Question**:
- **How long had** you **been working** before the meeting?
- **Had** they **been waiting** long?

#### Past Perfect Continuous vs Past Perfect
| Past Perfect Continuous | Past Perfect |
|-------------------------|--------------|
- I had been working for 3 hours when he called. | - I had worked for 3 hours by the time he called. |
- She had been studying all day. | - She had studied all the material. |

**Catatan**: Past Perfect Continuous lebih fokus pada DURASI dan PROSES berkelanjutan

### 9. Future Simple

#### Rumus
| Subject | Formula |
|---------|---------|
| All subjects | will + base verb (V1) |

**Negative**: won't (will not) + base verb
**Question**: Will + subject + base verb?

#### Penggunaan
1. **Predictions** (prediksi masa depan)
   - It **will rain** tomorrow.
   - She **will be** successful in her career.

2. **Spontaneous Decisions** (keputusan spontan saat berbicara)
   - The phone is ringing. I **will answer** it.
   - I forgot my umbrella. I **will buy** a new one.

3. **Promises & Offers** (janji & tawaran)
   - I **will help** you with the project.
   - We **will do** our best.

4. **Threats & Warnings** (ancaman & peringatan)
   - You **will regret** this decision.
   - I **will report** you if you continue.

5. **First Conditional** (conditional sentence type 1)
   - If it **rains**, I **will stay** at home.

#### Time Markers
- tomorrow, next week/month/year
- soon, later, in the future
- in 2 days, in a week

#### Contoh Lengkap
‚úÖ **Positive**:
- I **will finish** the report tomorrow.
- She **will arrive** at 3 PM.

‚ùå **Negative**:
- They **won't come** to the meeting.
- He **won't be** able to attend.

‚ùì **Question**:
- **Will** you **join** us for lunch?
- **Will** she **accept** the offer?

#### Will vs Going to
| Will | Going to |
|------|----------|
| Prediksi, keputusan spontan | Rencana, niat, prediksi berdasarkan bukti |
- I think it will rain. | - Look at those clouds. It's going to rain. |
- I'll help you. (spontan) | - I'm going to help you. (rencana) |

### 13. Future in the Past

Used to express future actions from a past perspective.

#### Rumus
| Tense | Formula |
|-------|---------|
| Future Simple (Past) | would + base verb |

2. **Plans from Past Perspective** (rencana dari perspektif masa lalu)
   - She said she **would come** the next day.
   - They promised they **would finish** the project.

#### Contoh Lengkap
‚úÖ **Future Simple in Past**:
- I said I **would help** him.
- She said she **would call** me later.

‚úÖ **Future Continuous in Past**:
- He said he **would be waiting** for me.
- I thought you **would be working** at that time.

‚úÖ **Future Perfect in Past**:
- She thought she **would have finished** by then.
- They believed the project **would have been completed**.

## üéØ TOEIC TENSES TRICKS & TIPS

### Common TOEIC Tense Questions

#### **1. Time Marker Clues**
- "yesterday" ‚Üí Past Simple ‚ùå I work ‚Üí ‚úÖ I worked
- "since 2020" ‚Üí Present Perfect ‚ùå I work ‚Üí ‚úÖ I have worked
- "at 3 PM tomorrow" ‚Üí Future Continuous ‚ùå I work ‚Üí ‚úÖ I will be working
- "by next Friday" ‚Üí Future Perfect ‚ùå I will work ‚Üí ‚úÖ I will have worked

#### **2. Sequence of Events**
- "After she **had finished**, she **went** home." (Past Perfect then Past Simple)
- "When I **arrived**, they **were having** lunch." (Past Simple then Past Continuous)

#### **3. Reported Speech**
- "He said he **would** come." (Future in the Past)
- "She said she **had finished** the report." (Past Perfect)

#### **4. Conditional Sentences**
- Type 1: If it **rains**, I **will stay** home.
- Type 2: If I **were** you, I **would accept** the offer.
- Type 3: If I **had known**, I **would have helped**.

### Quick Reference for TOEIC

| Situation | Use Tense |
|-----------|-----------|
| Facts/Habits | Present Simple |
| Happening now | Present Continuous |
| Finished, time not specified | Present Perfect |
| Started in past, still continuing | Present Perfect Continuous |
| Finished, time specified | Past Simple |
| Was happening at past time | Past Continuous |
| Finished before another past action | Past Perfect |
| Continuing until past action | Past Perfect Continuous |
| Prediction/Spontaneous decision | Future Simple (will) |
| Arranged plan | Present Continuous (I'm meeting...) |
| In progress at future time | Future Continuous |
| Completed before future time | Future Perfect |

### Common TOEIC Mistakes

1. **Wrong Tense with Time Marker**
   - ‚ùå Yesterday I work. ‚Üí ‚úÖ Yesterday I worked.
   - ‚ùå Since 2020 I worked. ‚Üí ‚úÖ Since 2020 I have worked.

2. **Confusing Tense Forms**
   - ‚ùå I am knowing him. ‚Üí ‚úÖ I know him.
   - ‚ùå I am understanding. ‚Üí ‚úÖ I understand.

3. **Sequence Errors**
   - ‚ùå After she finished, she goes home. ‚Üí ‚úÖ After she had finished, she went home.

4. **Passive Voice with Tenses**
   - ‚ùå The report was write yesterday. ‚Üí ‚úÖ The report was written yesterday.
   - ‚ùå The project has complete. ‚Üí ‚úÖ The project has been completed.

## üìù Practice Tips

### How to Master Tenses

1. **Identify Time Markers**
   - Look for words like: yesterday, tomorrow, for 2 hours, since 2020, by next week
   - These words tell you which tense to use

2. **Understand the Context**
   - Is the action finished? Still continuing? Future?
