## Vocabulary Learning Tips

1. **Learn words in context** - Don't just memorize definitions
2. **Use flashcards** - Create digital or physical flashcards
3. **Read business materials** - Business news, emails, reports
4. **Practice regularly** - Review vocabulary daily
5. **Use the words** - Try to use new vocabulary in sentences
