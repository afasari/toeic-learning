## Listening Section Tips

1. **Stay 100% focused** - No distractions allowed
2. **Listen for key information** - Numbers, names, dates, locations
3. **Pay attention to tone** - Speaker's attitude can indicate meaning
4. **Don't panic** - If you miss something, keep going
5. **Read questions first** - Especially for Parts 3 and 4

## Common Listening Distractors

- **Similar words** - Words that sound alike but have different meanings
- **Wrong information** - Details that are mentioned but not the answer
- **Opposite meanings** - Negative or contrary statements
- **Irrelevant details** - True but not what's being asked
